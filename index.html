Problem & Solution

We are solving the Africa's biggest blockchain issues surrounding cross-border payments, data accessibility and payment.

Evolution of Cryptocurrency

The adoption of cryptocurrencies has been on the rise and we are beginning to see widespread use of cryptocurrencies not just for speculators but for everyday users who transact value equivalent to millions of dollars daily.

Problem in Africa

Traditionally, Africa has lagged behind when new technology has been introduced in the developed world to aid business strategy. But with cryptocurrencies, it is obvious that cryptocurrency users in Africa need a better payment system that can help them accept cryptocurrencies as seamlessly as cash and card payments, while hedging against the risk of price fluctuations due to cryptocurrency volatility.

That All Changes
with PayAfrik

There are a number of solutions created to enable Africans use cryptocurrencies with ease, but the greatest challenge has been the business loss from cryptocurrency payments declining in value.

Enter PayAfrik with a novel solution to this problem by helping merchants and users utilise cryptocurrencies for payments by engaging with traditional banking and payment services while eliminating the risk of price fluctuations.

PayAfrik offers a cryptocurrency wallet with which users can accept cryptocurrencies for services rendered & pay with cryptocurrencies for services received. The PayAfrik wallet can receive and send value anywhere.

P2P and B2B Lending of Crypto Assets

AfriCoin (AFK) — The PayAfrik native token

AFK is the native PayAfrik token that users can use to exchange value in the PayAfrik ecosystem. The token can be used as collateral to secure loans and other credit in the PayAfrik ecosystem.
